⚔ Magic & Might ⚔

A Heroes of Might and Magic Clone

How to Play

  • Click on the map to move your hero
  • Enter to end your turn
  • T to open town (when hero is at a town)
  • Tab to cycle between heroes
  • Esc to close town view
  • In combat: click enemies to attack, empty cells to move
  • W to wait, D to defend, A for auto-battle

Goal

Build your army, capture towns and mines, and defeat the enemy!
